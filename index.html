About Me

I am a 2rd-year Master student in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University, China working with Prof. Xuming He. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from ShanghaiTech University, China in 2021.

My research interests broadly include Computer Vision and Deep Learning, with a focus on scene understanding and low-level vision using strong or weak supervision in the open world scenario. I am especially interested in robust learning, segmentation, uncertainty estimation, out-of-distribution detection, and out-of-distribution generalization. I am looking for a Ph.D. position in relevant fields in Fall 2024.
